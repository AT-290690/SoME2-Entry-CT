Lesson 1 : What is category

During our journey in the country of abstraction, we met something. Its name is $\C$ and it pretends to be a category. What does it mean? What can we do with it?

The data required for $\C$ to be called a category

To be a category, $\C$ has to possess a bunch of objects. Those objects can be anything and we assume that they form a collection called $\ob \C$. Double click anywhere on the screen to create a new object of $\C$.
